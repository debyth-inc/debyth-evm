# Debyth Mandate - User Guide

## What is Debyth Mandate?

Think of Debyth Mandate like setting up automatic bill payments, but for crypto. You can set up recurring payments (like monthly subscriptions) that happen automatically, while you stay in complete control of your money.

## Why Use Debyth Mandate?

### The Problem We Solve
- **Manual payments are annoying**: You have to remember to pay every month
- **Traditional crypto approvals are risky**: You either give unlimited access (scary!) or have to approve every single payment (annoying!)
- **Payments often fail**: You forget to top up your approval and your subscription gets canceled

### Our Solution
- **Set it and forget it**: Create a payment plan once, it runs automatically
- **Smart approval management**: We tell you exactly when and how much to approve
- **Never lose control**: You can stop payments instantly anytime
- **No surprises**: Get warnings before anything goes wrong

## How It Works (Simple Version)

### Step 1: Set Up Your Payment Plan
Just like setting up a monthly Netflix subscription, you tell us:
- Who to pay (the merchant)
- How much to pay each time (like $10 per month)
- How often to pay (weekly, monthly, etc.)
- When to start and stop

### Step 2: Approve Your Tokens
Instead of giving unlimited access to your money, we calculate exactly how much you need for the next few payments and ask you to approve just that amount (plus a small buffer for safety).

**Example**: If you want to pay $10 monthly for 6 months, we'll ask you to approve around $66 ($60 + 10% buffer).

### Step 3: Relax and Get Notified
- Payments happen automatically on schedule
- We monitor your approval balance
- When you're running low (like 3 payments left), we send you a friendly reminder
- If you're critically low (1 payment left), we pause everything to protect you

### Step 4: Top Up When Needed
When you get a low balance warning:
- We tell you exactly how much to approve
- You approve that amount
- Payments resume automatically
- No stress, no failed payments

## Real-World Example

**Sarah wants to pay for her monthly crypto newsletter ($5 USDC per month):**

1. **Setup**: Sarah creates a mandate to pay the newsletter $5 USDC every 30 days for 1 year
2. **Smart Approval**: We calculate she needs about $66 USDC (12 payments × $5 + 10% buffer)
3. **Sarah Approves**: She approves $66 USDC - much safer than unlimited approval
4. **Auto Payments**: Every month, $5 gets paid automatically
5. **Smart Monitoring**: After 9 payments, we warn Sarah she has 3 payments left
6. **Easy Top-up**: Sarah approves another $33 USDC for the remaining payments
7. **Peace of Mind**: Sarah never worries about failed payments or unlimited approvals

## Your Control Options

### You Can Always:
- **Stop Everything**: Cancel your payment plan instantly
- **Emergency Stop**: Remove all approvals to stop payments immediately
- **Customize Warnings**: Set when you want to be warned (default: 3 payments remaining)
- **Disable Auto-Pause**: Turn off automatic pausing if you prefer (not recommended)
- **Resume Payments**: Unpause after topping up your approval

### You'll Get Notified When:
- ⚠️ **Low Balance Warning**: "You have 3 payments left, consider topping up with $33"
- 🚨 **Critical Alert**: "Only 1 payment left! Top up with $33 to avoid auto-pause"
- ⏸️ **Auto-Pause**: "Payment paused for your protection. Top up and unpause to continue"
- ✅ **Payment Success**: "Monthly payment of $5 completed successfully"

## Safety Features

### We Protect You By:
- **Never asking for unlimited approvals**: You only approve what you need
- **Monitoring constantly**: We check your balance after every payment
- **Pausing when needed**: If you're about to run out, we pause to protect you
- **Warning in advance**: You get plenty of notice before anything happens
- **Keeping you in control**: You can stop, pause, or modify anything anytime

### You're Protected From:
- **Unlimited spending**: We never take more than you approved
- **Failed payments**: Our monitoring prevents surprise failures
- **Forgotten subscriptions**: You set the end date, payments stop automatically
- **Hacks or exploits**: Limited approvals mean limited exposure

## Common Questions

### "What if I want to stop paying?"
Just cancel your mandate - it takes one click and stops all future payments immediately.

### "What if I forget to top up my approval?"
We'll pause your payments automatically to protect you. No failed payments, no penalties.

### "What if the service I'm paying raises their prices?"
The payment will fail (we can't pay more than your limit), and you'll need to create a new mandate with the higher amount.

### "Can I pay multiple services?"
Yes! You can create as many payment plans as you want, each with its own approval balance.

### "What if I lose access to my wallet?"
Since you control the approvals, losing wallet access automatically stops all payments - your money stays safe.
